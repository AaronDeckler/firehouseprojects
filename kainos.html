Our biggest yearly gathering of folks interested in community in the Grace & Glory of Jesus' Finished Work!

This year in Yellow Spring, West Virginia!

Every year on Labor Day weekend, Matt & Katie Spinks, Jared Gustafson, and friends host a 3 day family re-union Glory party for the sake of empowering one another towards new wineskins of healthy growing church life.

If you are wondering which gathering you want to attend this year, THIS is the one! Not just a conference, but relational empowerment for living a lifestyle in the Bliss of Jesus!

We will worship, hang out, have some interactive seminars, and get wild and rowdy in the Good Lord!

Additional Information

Our meals are always made by our group together (part of the community fun!) and we usually have healthy and delicious options at every meal, as well as things kids like.

RVs and Camping Trailers are welcome! However, there are no hook ups. Please message us for pricing. Those who register early will be given more preference if possible.

We will not be offering refunds on registration after August 1st, before that, just shoot us a message.

There is a swimming pool!

There are hiking trails, and the possibility of several kinds of recreational activities!

No alcohol or non-prescribed drugs allowed on the premises.

Feel free to bring an instrument!

We may be able to arrange carpooling, send us a message and we will try our best! We want to help you get there!

The closest big airport is Washington Dulles.
