We want to welcome you to Kainos Koinonia 2025!!!

This is our yearly gathering for Jesus mystics and Trinitarian Gospel junkies who are interested in growing in what it means to do community and church!

Since 2015, Matt Spinks, Jared Gustafson and like-minded communities have been organizing Kainos Koinonia once a year in different parts of North America. The goal was to eat meals, worship and share conversation together for days on end toward (Kainos) New Creation Community (Koinonia), in tha Glory!
